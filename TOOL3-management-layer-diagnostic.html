The restructuring reality — what most leaders do not know
Gartner tracked 1.4 million layoffs in 2025 and found that less than 1% were actually caused by AI productivity gains. Many organizations are using AI as cover for restructuring they wanted to implement for other reasons — creating significant risk of hollowing out leadership pipelines without genuine strategic design. Additionally, flattened organizations often exhibit more centralized control at the top — the exact opposite of the intended goal.
